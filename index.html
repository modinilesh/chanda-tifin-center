💛 Our Story

Why Chanda?

🌸

"Chanda is not just a name. She is my mother."

This kitchen is built in her honour — inspired by her love, her care, and the way she feeds everyone like they are family. Every tifin we pack carries that same warmth she always cooked with.

When you order from Chanda Tifin Center, you are not getting restaurant food or packaged meals. You are getting the kind of food a mother makes — with fresh ingredients, real spices, and a heart full of love. No artificial taste. No shortcuts. Only ghar ka swaad.

We cap at just 50 tifins a day — not because we can't do more, but because Maa always said: "Jab pyaar se banana ho, toh jaldi mein mat banana." (When you cook with love, never rush.)
